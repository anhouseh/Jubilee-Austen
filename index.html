A little bit about me.

California based web developer. My journey into tech started during the pandemic. Working in a medical administrative position during the height of the pandemic, I was underpaid and overutilized, I craved for a change. My workplace offered employees a limited opportunity to enroll in free online courses. Exploring web development opened curiousity, excitement and opportunity.

Taking my first HTML class and seeing my first code for “Hello World” was a springboard for my interest into web development and eventually enrolled in coding school. That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. I love being able to create something out of nothing and seeing something tangible. Web development has made me feel capable, proud, and accomplished. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, organized code.
